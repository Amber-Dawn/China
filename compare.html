* Taiwan vs China *

Taiwan is better than China!!!!


And here's why:


- China may have a bigger population, but their total nation income is only 10 times greater.

- In terms of PPP, Taiwan ranks38th in the world whereas China ranks 133rd.

- In Taiwan, 57% of the population has access to the internet. In China, only 19% of the population has access to the internet.

- In China, the amount of people who live under poverty is 8% of the population. In Taiwan it is only 0.95% of the population.
